API must be enabled in Mylar3 settings.

Allowed fields: `["series", "issues", "wanted"]`.

```yaml
widget:
    type: mylar
    url: http://mylar3.host.or.ip:port
    key: yourmylar3apikey
```

*Added in v0.6.7*
